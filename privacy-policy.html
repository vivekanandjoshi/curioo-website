Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

Curioo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our educational app.

Information We Collect

Parent/Guardian Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Phone number (optional, for account recovery)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay)

Child Information:

  • First name or nickname (for personalization)
  • Age/birth year (to provide age-appropriate content)
  • Learning progress and activity data

How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized educational content
  • Track learning progress and achievements
  • Improve our app and content
  • Send important updates to parents
  • Process subscription payments

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We:

  • Require parental consent before collecting any child's personal information
  • Do not collect more information than necessary
  • Do not share children's information with third parties for marketing
  • Allow parents to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your data. Our backend is hosted on Supabase with enterprise-grade security.

Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Razorpay: Payment processing (India)
  • Firebase: Push notifications

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@curioo.in

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.
